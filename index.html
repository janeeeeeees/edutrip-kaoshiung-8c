About Taiwan

So, if you are wondering or confused you guys might ask, what exactly is Taiwan, what to do in Taiwan, and what did I do in Taiwan? To answer all these questions, I'll briefly explain about the beautiful country Taiwan.

Taiwan is situated in the West Pacific between Japan and the Philippines. The total area of Taiwan and its outlying islands is around 36,197 square kilometers with a population of 23.13 million, Taiwan is more populous than three-quarters of the world's nations. The country is mostly known for its vibrant culture, delicious cuisine and thriving economy, with Taipei being its capital. Taipei, the city that is known as the city that never sleeps, has a population of around 8 million people. It's not just about the city that makes it special, but here they also have many cultures, food, historical places, and many more.

Things to Do in Taiwan

Now here are some things to do in Taiwan to make your trip more worth it and fun! First, if you're interested in foods, you should go to street night markets such as Raohe Street Night Market and Ximending Street Night Market. Raohe Street Night Market is more perfect for people who wants to try interesting foods from Taiwan meanwhile Ximending Night Market is more perfect for shopping or gifts to buy from Taiwan.
